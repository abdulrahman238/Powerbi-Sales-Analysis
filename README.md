# Power BI Sales Analysis

## Overview
This project is a Power BI sales analysis dashboard built using 5 years of data (2018–2022) from a US electronics company.  
The report provides insights into revenue, customers, employee performance, products, and sales trends.

---

## Business Context
- Electronics company operating across the USA
- 21 product categories
- 80+ sales employees
- 5 years of sales data

---

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Microsoft Excel
- Power Query
- DAX

---

## Data Preparation & Modeling
- Loaded customer, product, sales, employee, and transaction data
- Cleaned data by removing empty rows
- Created relationships between tables
- Added calculated columns:
  - Unit Cost
  - Order Value = Quantity × Unit Price
- Formatted revenue values in USD

---

## Dashboard Pages

### Page 1: Key Data Points & Employee Performance
- Total Revenue by Year
- Total Customers by Year
- Total Employees
- Total States Served
- Total Products
- Total Product Categories
- Employee performance by revenue and quantity
- Year, Quarter, and Employee slicers

---

### Page 2: Top & Bottom Items
- Top 5 and Bottom 5 Products by Revenue
- Top 5 and Bottom 5 Categories by Revenue
- Top 5 and Bottom 5 Sales Employees by Revenue
- Top 5 and Bottom 5 States by Revenue
- Year slicer with single selection

---

### Page 3: AI Visuals
- Revenue comparison by quarter (Average)
- Key Influencer visual to identify revenue drivers
- Smart Narrative summary
- Q&A visual for user questions

---
